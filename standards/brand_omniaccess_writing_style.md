# OmniAccess — Writing Style & Consistency Standard

The house style for all official OmniAccess communications and documents.

## Language & tone

- Use **British English** spelling throughout.
- Refer to the company as **"OmniAccess"** (see the Brand Name standard).
- Describe OmniAccess's worldwide reach as **"Global company with local focus"**
  (lead with "Global").
- Prefer **"Our people"** rather than "team".

## Capitalisation

- Use a capital letter on the **first word only** (sentence case), except for:
  - The slogan **"Delivering Possibilities Anywhere"**
  - Product names
  - Official titles

## Punctuation & symbols

- Write the word **"and"** in full in official communications; use an ampersand
  (**&**) **only in titles**.
- Write **"percent"** as the **"%"** sign.
- Use the **Oxford comma** — a comma before "and"/"or" in a list of three or more
  items (e.g. "shipping, mining, and cruise").
- Introduce lists with a colon ( : ), not an en-dash ( – ).
- Do **not** put a full stop at the end of bullet points.

## Numbers, dates & units

- **Dates:** `20 March 2023` format (day, full month name, year).
- **Measurements:** add a space between the number and the unit — e.g. `60 cm`,
  `65 cm`, `80 cm`, `1 m` antennas.
- **Bit rates / throughput:** abbreviate as **Kbps** / **Mbps** (capital "K" / "M",
  per second) with a space before the unit — e.g. `up to 3 Mbps`, `1024 Kbps`.
- **Data sizes:** same spacing, capital abbreviations — e.g. `40 KB`, `60 GB`.
