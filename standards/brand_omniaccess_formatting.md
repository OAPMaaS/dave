# OmniAccess — Document Formatting & Layout Standard

How OmniAccess corporate documents (manuals, proposals, sales templates) must be
formatted. Based on the official OmniAccess manual template. Start every new
document from the template and copy your content into it to stay on-brand.

## Fonts

| Element | Font | Size | Colour |
|---|---|---|---|
| Heading 1 | IBM Plex Sans Medium | 15 pt | Black |
| Heading 2 | IBM Plex Sans Medium | 13 pt | Black |
| Heading 3 / Title 3 | IBM Plex Sans Medium | 12 pt | Black |
| Heading 4 / Title 4 (and deeper) | IBM Plex Sans Medium | 12 pt | Black |
| Body copy | IBM Plex Sans Light | 11.5 pt | Black |

- **IBM Plex Sans Medium** is used for all headline applications.
- **IBM Plex Sans Light** is used for all body copy.
- Heading spacing is consistent across all levels (use Heading 1 spacing).

## Brand colours

Use the custom RGB values — do not approximate:

| Colour | RGB | Use |
|---|---|---|
| OmniAccess Anthracite | R 30, G 41, B 52 | Highlights, table header background |
| OmniAccess Red | R 193, G 3, B 47 | Highlights / emphasis |

To highlight a piece of information, use the custom RGB colour in **bold**. (In Word:
Font Colour → More Colours if the custom red is not in the recent list.)

## Document structure

Every manual/proposal must contain, in order:

1. **Cover** — title, Date, Version, Ref.
2. **Table of Contents.**
3. **Revision history** table with columns: Revision, Date, Updated by, Description.
4. **Introduction** (Heading 1), then the body using the heading hierarchy above.
5. **"Need support?"** closing section pointing to the brand portal /
   `marketing@omniaccess.com`.

## Tables

- Main colour is **OmniAccess Anthracite**; header text in IBM Plex Sans Bold 10 pt.
- Use a light-grey background on every second row for readability.
- The change-tracking table uses columns: Issue/Rev., Date, Modification Nb,
  Modified Pages, Observations.

## Pictures & screenshots

- Insert screenshots/pictures with or without a frame, consistent within a document.
- Keep image resolution high enough to remain legible at print size.

## Lists

- Use a colon ( : ) to introduce a list — never an en-dash ( – ).
- Do **not** put a full stop at the end of bullet points.
- Bullet hierarchy and "letter i" / lower-case-letter styles are available in the
  template; keep one style consistent per list.
